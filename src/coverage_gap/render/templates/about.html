About

The Coverage Gap Index is built by Tenens, a public benefit corporation focused on physician supply for rural America.

Sixty million Americans in rural areas face severe specialist shortages. The locum tenens industry exists to bridge these gaps, but the institutions making 5 to 30 year capital decisions about physician supply (medical schools, federal workforce programs, hospital capital planning) work from data that is years stale and aggregated to the state level. Tenens builds the granular real-time signal those institutions need.

This index is the first artifact in that effort. It exists to prove the gap data is buildable from public sources and specific enough to drive operational decisions.
